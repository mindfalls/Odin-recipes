A simple lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g minced meat
  • a packet of pasta plates
  • 1 can od tomato sauce
  • 1 can of mushrooms
  • cheese
  • 1-4 garlic cloaves
  • 1 Yellow onion

Preparation

  1. Cut the onion
  2. fry the mince with the onion
  3. add tomato sauce,mushrooms and garlic
  4. let it simmer
  5. add pasta plates and cheese with the sauce to a pot in layers
  6. cook it in an oven at 200 degrees for 30 minutes
